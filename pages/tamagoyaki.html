Tamagoyaki

~ Japanese food ~

Tamagoyaki is Japanese rolled omelettes. It is a quite common food in Japan. Japanese usually eat it for breakfast or lunch. You can make it with just eggs, sugar, soy source, and oil.
Then, let's cook!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon soy source
  • oil

Preparation

  1. In a medium bowl, combine eggs, salt, sugar and soy sauce. Mix until everything is dissolved.
  2. Heat a tamagoyaki pan* over medium-high heat. Drip a little oil into the pan.
    *If you don't have a tamagoyaki pan, you can use a frying pan.
  3. Pour a third of the egg mixture into pan and quickly swirl to cover the entire pan.
  4. When the egg is half-set, gently roll the egg.
  5. With the rolled egg still in the pan, pour in another third of the egg mixture. When the egg is half-set, roll the omelette toward you. Repeat with the rest of the egg mixture.
  6. Slice into bite-sized pieces.
