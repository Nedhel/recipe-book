Aguachile

~ Mexican dish ~

Aguachile is a Mexican dish, mostly eaten in the north coast states, but it is known in all Mexico. We eat it as the main dish with crispy tortilla

Ingredients

      1/2 kg Shrimps
      1/4 Limes
      Half Purple Onion
      1 Cucumber
      a few Cilantro
      2 or 3 pieces green Chili
      5 Crispy tortilla
      1 Avocado

Preparation

  1. Peel the shrimps and put them into a bowl.
  2. Pours the Lime juice in the bowl and wait around 15 or 20 minuts.
  3. Mix in a blender, a few lime juice with cilantro and chili, then put it in the bowl as well.
  4. Cut the cucumber and the purple onion into slice and mix with shrimps.
  5. Put a little of that mix on crispy tortilla and avocado on top and enjoy!!
