Horchata beverage

~ Mexican Beverange ~

This is a typical beverage in many places in Mexico, is fresh and deliciuos

Ingredients

      1 cup Rice
      1 lt Water
      1 stick Cinnamon
      Half can of Condensed milk
      Sugar like you want

Preparation

  1. Leave the rice and cinnamon in one liter of water overnight.
  2. In the morning put them in a blender with condensed milk and blend them.
  3. After that you need to pass the mixture through a filter and only keep the liquid.
  4. Mix that liquid with more water add some ice and enjoy it!!
